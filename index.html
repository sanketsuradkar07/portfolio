Expense Tracker

I developed 'Expense Tracker'. The Expense Tracker was a group project developed to help users manage their daily expenses in a simple and effective way. The system allows users to record their income and expenses, organize transactions into different categories, and keep track of their financial activities in an easy-to-understand format. It includes useful features such as budget management, expense summaries, and basic visual reports that help users analyze their spending patterns and make better financial decisions.

Takeaways: This project marked a significant step in my journey as a developer. Working collaboratively enriched my understanding of teamwork, UI design, and web technologies.

Smart OPD System

I developed 'Smart OPD System' which is a digital healthcare solution designed to simplify and improve the outpatient department (OPD) process in hospitals. It allows patients to book appointments online, reducing long waiting times and overcrowding in hospitals. The system provides features like doctor scheduling, patient registration, and real-time appointment tracking, making the process more organized and efficient. Patients receive notifications and reminders, which helps them manage their visits better.

Takeaways: Smart OPD System is an online platform that streamlines hospital appointments, reduces waiting time, and improves patient and doctor interaction.
